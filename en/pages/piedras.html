Piedras del Sol is a boutique inn located in the heart of the mountains of Córdoba.

Design: Camila Dallaglio - Caminito ediciones

Project Details

Piedras del Sol is an exclusive inn with only 10 rooms. Among its services are the outdoor pool, indoor covered pool, relaxation area with whirlpools, and much more.
Our goal was to convey, through social media, the same feeling one experiences while staying here: a unique place where relaxation and nature converge, where attention to detail is paramount, and where everything is prepared for the guest's maximum comfort.
